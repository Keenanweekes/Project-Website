South Coast Private CRM Software Tool

Our Project

CRM Technologies has been selected to develop a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system on behalf of South Coast Private Hospital (the Client).

The development of this CRM will see the digital transformation of work processes associated with Marketing and Business Development activities. The CRM will specifically handle the management of prospective and current provider information in which the hospital utilises for engagement and educational purposes.

Further information about the CRM solution and its benefits can be found on the Solution page.

Our Client

South Coast Private Hospital (SCP) is a private mental health hospital located in Wollongong. The hospital offers a variety of inpatient and outpatient programs to treat a range of psychiatric conditions including trauma, anxiety and addictions.

SCP has over 200 employees including GPs, psychiatrists, nurses and allied health professionals and is a subsidary of Healthe Care Pty Ltd, the third largest private hospital operator in Australia.

Our Philosophy

CRM Technologies has adopted an agile approach to project development for its proven ability to deliver project objectives efficiently and in close alignment with the needs of the Client and system Users.

The agile project principles that CRM Technologies have adopted include:

  • Delivering software components at regular intervals
  • Collaborating regularly with the Client to monitor satisfaction
  • Responding to changing project requirements to deliver an optimal solution

Our Team

CRM Technologies is a group of seven University of Wollongong students completing their final year project. The group was established for the purpose of this project and is formed by students with a set of complementary skills including - systems analysis and design, software engineering, human computer interaction, e-business technology, cyber security, law and mobile and game development.

An overview of team members and their project roles and responsibilities can be found on the Team Members page.
