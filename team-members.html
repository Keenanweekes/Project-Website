Our Team

CRM Technologies is a group of seven University of Wollongong students completing their final year project. The group was established for the purpose of this project and is formed by students with a set of complementary skills including - systems analysis and design, software engineering, human computer interaction, e-business technology, cyber security, law and mobile and game development.
